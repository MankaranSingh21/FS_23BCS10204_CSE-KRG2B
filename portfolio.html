About Internship

I worked as a data analyst intern at Bharti Airtel from 19th May 2025 to 30th June 2025. I learnt more about DBT (Data Build Tool) and how it helps in building elaborate data warehouses. Moving away from basic SQL, I was tasked with building a Data Purging Model to aid the efficient removal of redundant and old data.
